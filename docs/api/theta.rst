Theta Functions
===============

The Jacobi theta functions $\theta_2(q)$, $\theta_3(q)$, and $\theta_4(q)$
are fundamental objects in the theory of elliptic functions and modular
forms. They satisfy the Jacobi triple product identity and numerous
transformation formulas.
